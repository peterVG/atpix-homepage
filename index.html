ATPix is not a polished consumer app. It is a developer-focused PoC for user-owned PDS storage, C2PA 2.2 upload provenance, and permissioned albums via HappyView Spaces (ATP-0016). Path A (own uploads → My Gallery) ships in the monorepo today; Path B network discovery and public profile sharing follow after permissioned-space validation.
